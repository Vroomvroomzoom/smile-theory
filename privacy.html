Privacy Policy.

A clear summary of how this website handles privacy, contact links, and basic technical information.

Effective April 2026 Website-only policy

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Smile Theory Dental Clinic website. It explains how this website handles information when you browse pages or click links that open phone, email, WhatsApp, Instagram, or map services.

2. Information This Website Handles

This website is designed to collect as little information as possible.

  • No forms: the website does not currently collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, medical details, or appointment requests through an embedded form.
  • No tracking: the website does not use analytics, advertising pixels, cookies, or third-party tracking scripts.
  • Basic server logs: our hosting provider may automatically generate standard technical logs, such as IP address, browser type, requested page, date/time, and error information, for security and reliability.
  • Link interactions: if you click phone, email, WhatsApp, Instagram, or map links, your browser or the external service may handle that interaction under its own privacy practices.

3. How Website Data Is Used

Any basic technical information generated by hosting logs may be used only to:

  • Keep the website available and secure.
  • Diagnose technical issues or broken pages.
  • Prevent abuse, spam, attacks, or misuse of the website.
  • Meet hosting, security, or legal obligations where applicable.

4. Contact Links

The website includes links to call, email, WhatsApp, Instagram, and maps. Clicking those links may open apps or third-party websites outside our control. Any information you choose to send through those services is handled by that service and, where applicable, by the clinic team receiving your message.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not set cookies for analytics, advertising, remarketing, or visitor profiling. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated to explain what is used and why.

6. Third-Party Services

Some links on this website may take you to third-party services. These may include:

  • WhatsApp for messaging.
  • Your phone or email app for calls and emails.
  • Instagram for social links.
  • Google Maps or another maps provider for directions.

These services are not controlled by Smile Theory Dental Clinic. Please review their privacy terms if you want to understand how they handle your information.

7. Data Retention

We do not maintain a website visitor database. Technical server logs, if generated by our hosting provider, are retained only as needed for security, troubleshooting, reliability, and hosting operations.

8. Security

We keep the website intentionally lightweight and avoid unnecessary data collection. We also use reasonable website and hosting safeguards to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, or disruption.

9. Your Choices

You can browse this website without submitting personal information through the site. If you do not want to share information through third-party apps, you can contact the clinic directly by phone instead.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this website privacy policy if we add forms, booking tools, analytics, cookies, or other website features. The effective date above reflects the current version.

Contact

For questions about this website privacy policy, contact our administrative team.
